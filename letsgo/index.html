Complete this checklist before responding to any new opportunity. Any disqualifying answer will flag the lead for decline.

Opportunity Details
Brief description of the prospect, their ask, and the proposed engagement.
BANT Criteria
1. Budget Confirmed
Has the prospect confirmed an allocated budget?
2. Budget Threshold
Estimated deal size (USD).
3. Authority
Was the conversation with a decision-maker holding direct purchasing authority?
4. Need
Has a concrete business need been clearly articulated and confirmed?
5. Timing
Is the opportunity expected to close within the current or next quarter?
